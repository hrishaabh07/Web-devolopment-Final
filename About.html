About Middlesex University

Middlesex University is a dynamic and diverse institution, dedicated to delivering high-quality education and research. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, knowledge, and experiences they need to succeed in a global society.

Our History

Founded in 1878, Middlesex University has a long history of academic excellence. Over the years, we have evolved into one of the leading universities in the UK, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional development programs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a world-class university that delivers impactful research and transformative education. We aim to inspire and support students from all walks of life to achieve their fullest potential.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We are committed to the highest standards of academic and professional conduct.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and work towards an inclusive and supportive community.
  • Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation and creativity, encouraging new ideas and approaches.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from teaching to research.

Our Campus

Our modern campus offers state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant, welcoming environment for students from across the globe. Located in the heart of London, the campus is surrounded by cultural, social, and professional opportunities.
